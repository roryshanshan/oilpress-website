## Return And Order Issue Handling

- Equipment on this site is supplied against factory quotation, technical confirmation, and the signed contract. Many projects are confirmed against voltage, capacity, packing method, and process requirements before production.
- Before placing an order, please confirm the model, configuration, lead time, packing method, and service scope with our sales team.
- If you find transport damage, quantity discrepancies, or a mismatch between the delivered equipment and the confirmed contract or order, please contact our sales and after-sales support team as soon as possible.
- For quality-related after-sales handling, please refer to the service commitments on this page and our published quality commitments. Warranty coverage and execution follow the contract and the public project information.
