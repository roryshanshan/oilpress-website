# GFP-12B low vacuum filling machine

![GFP-12B low vacuum filling machine](/images/filling-products/gfp-12b-low-vacuum-filling-machine.webp)

`GFP-12B` is a 12-head model in the low vacuum constant level filling series. Public information shows that it is in the same production capacity segment as `GFP-12A` and is suitable for filling projects of small and medium-sized batches of wine, rice wine, soy sauce, vinegar and other easily foaming liquids.

## Core parameters

| Project | Parameters |
| --- | --- |
| Model | GFP-12B |
| Number of filling heads | 12 |
| Reference output | 3000 bottles/hour |
| Total power | 1.12 kW |
| Dimensions | 1045×1050×2200 mm |
| Filling method | Low vacuum/constant level filling |
| Applicable liquids | Wine, rice wine, soy sauce, vinegar and other easily foaming liquids |

## What does this device do?

GFP-12B is still a low vacuum liquid level filling solution, focusing on foam control, liquid level stabilization and bottle loss reduction. If your site is a small or medium-capacity liquor line or seasoning liquid line, and you want to retain space for subsequent plugging, cap shrinking, and labeling expansion, this type of 12-head model is usually a better choice.

## Public Features

- Liquid level filling, suitable for liquids that are airless but prone to foaming.
- Liquid flows down the bottle wall, helping to suppress foaming during filling.
- Compact structure, suitable for small and medium-sized production capacity workshop layout.
- The elastic bottle support structure can reduce the loss of glass bottles during the filling process.
- No filling without bottles helps reduce the probability of empty filling and unqualified bottles entering the subsequent process.
- The filling valve is relatively easy to disassemble and clean, and is suitable for routine cleaning needs of wine and flavoring liquids.

## Which projects are suitable for

- Liquor bottling project with a production capacity of approximately 3,000 bottles/hour.
- Filling of glass bottles with soy sauce, vinegar and other easily foaming liquids.
- Small and medium-sized customers who need to install a single machine first and then expand it to a full line later.
