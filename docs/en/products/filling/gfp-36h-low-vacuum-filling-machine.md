# GFP-36H low vacuum filling machine

![GFP-36H low vacuum filling machine](/images/filling-products/gfp-36h-low-vacuum-filling-machine.webp)

`GFP-36H` is a 36-head model in the low vacuum constant level filling series. It has entered the 10,000 bottles/hour level and is suitable for more mature wine, rice wine and glass bottle seasoning liquid packaging lines.

## Core parameters

| Project | Parameters |
| --- | --- |
| Model | GFP-36H |
| Number of filling heads | 36 |
| Reference output | 10,000 bottles/hour |
| Total power | 3 kW |
| Dimensions | 2500×2350×2200 mm |
| Filling method | Low vacuum/constant level filling |
| Applicable liquids | Wine, rice wine, soy sauce, vinegar and other easily foaming liquids |

## What does this device do?

GFP-36H corresponds to higher throughput low vacuum filling requirements. It not only retains the advantage of fixed liquid level appearance, but is also more suitable for customers who have requirements for shift production and rhythm. It is especially suitable for automatic plugging, cap shrinking, labeling, and cartoning to form a continuous production layout.

## Public Features

- Low vacuum pressure difference filling, suitable for stable filling of easily foaming liquids.
- Liquid flows down the bottle wall, which reduces impact bubbling.
- The filling valve has fewer seals and is more stable in the long term.
- The bottle holder has a soft structure, which helps reduce the loss of glass bottles.
- No filling without bottles, improving the quality consistency of finished products in the later stages.
- Easy to maintain and clean, suitable for mass production projects.

## Which projects are suitable for

- A large-scale filling line for wine or rice wine with a capacity of about 10,000 bottles/hour.
- High-frequency filling projects such as soy sauce and vinegar in glass bottles.
- Customers who have requirements for filling rhythm, liquid level consistency and back-end automation connection.
