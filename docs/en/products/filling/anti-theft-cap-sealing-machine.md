## Disclosure points

- This machine is suitable for bottles sealed with twist-off aluminum anti-theft caps. The sealing knife is made of special materials and undergoes strict heat treatment process. It has high strength, wear resistance and long service life. The rolling head adopts a four-wheel balanced working principle, which is simple and convenient to adjust. The rolling pattern and edge guarding are automatically completed in one go. The texture is even, the appearance is smooth and wrinkle-free, the sealing effect is good, and the efficiency is high. The fast-structure bottle dialing star wheel, bottle guide plate, and bottle feeding screw can be quickly inserted and replaced, which is convenient and simple. The bottle mouth is equipped with an automatic positioning device to avoid many adverse effects such as bottle inversion, bottle jamming, and inaccurate positioning of the bottle mouth. The center distance of the rolling head is just right, which reduces the centrifugal force phenomenon during normal operation. The surface of the guide bushing is strengthened and closely matched with the roller bearing, which reduces the working pulse, making the equipment free of vibration and impact at high speed, making the work more stable and reliable. Both the power system and the dial system are equipped with buffer overload protection devices, and elastic elements are installed to absorb the start-up, making it smooth and gentle. When an accident occurs, the overload device immediately cuts off the power supply and the equipment automatically shuts down, ensuring human and machine safety.
