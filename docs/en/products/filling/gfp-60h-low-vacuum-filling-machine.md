# GFP-60H low vacuum filling machine

![GFP-60H low vacuum filling machine](/images/filling-products/gfp-60h-low-vacuum-filling-machine.webp)

`GFP-60H` is a higher-capacity version of the low-vacuum constant-level filling series, targeting large-scale glass bottle liquid filling projects at the 20,000 bottles/hour level.

## Core parameters

| Project | Parameters |
| --- | --- |
| Model | GFP-60H |
| Number of filling heads | 60 |
| Reference output | 20,000 bottles/hour |
| Total power | 7.5 kW |
| Overall dimensions | 3500×3500×2200 mm |
| Filling method | Low vacuum/constant level filling |
| Applicable liquids | Wine, rice wine, soy sauce, vinegar and other easily foaming liquids |

## What does this device do?

GFP-60H mainly serves larger-scale low vacuum and constant liquid level filling needs. For customers who already have the foundation of pre-processing, filtration, conveying and post-packaging automation, this level is more suitable as a complete line filling host.

## Public Features

- Low vacuum liquid level filling solution, suitable for liquids that are prone to foaming.
- Liquid adheres to the wall and flows downward, which can reduce bubbling at the bottom.
- Reliable structure, suitable for high-intensity continuous production.
- The gentle action of holding the bottle helps protect the glass bottle.
- No bottle, no filling, easy to control the rhythm of the entire line.
- Easy to maintain and clean, suitable for daily maintenance of large-scale projects.

## Which projects are suitable for

- Complete line for flavored liquids in wine or glass bottles with a capacity of 20,000 bottles/hour.
- Customers who have higher automation requirements for back-end boxing, palletizing, and traceability.
- Factories that want to cover large-volume order production on the same filling platform.
