- Applicable scenarios: More suitable for medium-speed glass bottle and PET bottle rinsing.

## Summary of public information

This machine adopts the sleeve roller chain transmission principle. The entire conveyor chain has accurate transmission and good wear resistance. The circulating water uses tracking spraying and fixed pipe spraying. The clean water part uses a water distributor to track and spray to save water sources and improve water utilization.
