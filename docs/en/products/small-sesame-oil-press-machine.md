# Small Sesame Oil Press Machine

![Small sesame oil press machine](/images/korean-type-small-press/small-sesame-hero.jpg)

The small sesame oil press machine is specially positioned for high-quality sesame oil production in small batches. In most cases, it is based on the Korean type hydraulic oil press design and is used in sesame oil shops, organic oil stores, and compact commercial production spaces.

## Why this machine is best for sesame

Small Korean type hydraulic presses are optimized for premium, high-oil-content materials and shop-scale output. Sesame matches this family especially well because it offers:

- High oil yield
- Easy pressing characteristics
- Good suitability for low-temperature processing
- Strong market demand in fresh oil shops

That is why this type of machine is commonly recommended for sesame-focused retail businesses rather than general large-scale oilseed projects.

## Small sesame press vs industrial hydraulic press

| Item | Small Sesame Press | Industrial Hydraulic Press |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Capacity | 5-20 kg | 60-100 kg |
| Best material | Sesame | Multi oil seeds |
| Usage | Shop / Retail | Factory |
| Production mode | Small batch | Bulk production |
| Investment | Low | Medium to high |

## When this machine is not the first choice

Although the machine can technically handle some other premium materials, it is not the best option for heavy-duty bulk processing projects such as:

- Large peanut oil output
- Soybean industrial pressing
- Rapeseed or mustard bulk production
- Large flaxseed throughput
- Factory-scale multi-seed projects
