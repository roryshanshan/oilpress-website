# Sesame Oil Production Line Guide: Hot Press, Cold Press, and Equipment Setup

*Published: March 18, 2026*
*Category: Technical Knowledge*

Sesame oil is one of the most profitable products for hydraulic pressing because customers value aroma, color, and purity. A well-designed line can serve both traditional fragrant oil markets and premium retail buyers.

## Standard hot press process

The common flow is cleaning, roasting or steaming, hydraulic pressing, settling or filtration, and filling. Hot pressing is still the mainstream choice for stronger aroma and higher yield.

## Cold press option for premium markets

Cold pressed sesame oil is suitable when the target market wants lighter aroma, natural labeling, and higher retail price. The process needs tighter temperature control and stable batch management.

## Core equipment package

Pretreatment equipment, hydraulic press, filter press, storage tank, and filling equipment are the basic modules. For traditional sesame oil, roasting quality is as important as the press model.

## Key control points

Pay attention to seed cleanliness, roasting temperature, moisture, batch load, and filtration speed. Good sesame oil lines are built around consistency, not only output.

## Conclusion

A sesame oil project becomes more competitive when the line is configured for the exact product style you want to sell. The correct process is the foundation of both aroma and profit.
